If you would like to know more about visiting our gallery
or about the Renaissance, check out this section of our
site. Also, you can always contact our employees with a
question in any way convenient for you.

How can I buy tickets?

Due to too high demand, we do not sell tickets directly
on the site. Fill out the form, and we will contact you to
confirm your order.

Are guided tours available?

Yes, we offer guided tours for visitors interested in
delving deeper into the artwork on display. Our expert
guides will provide fascinating insights, historical
context, and anecdotes about the artists and their
creations. Contact us to learn about the price and
schedule for guided tours.

Do you have parking facilities?

RenArt Gallery has its own parking facilities. Please
contact our gallery for information on parking
availability, rates, and all alternative transportation
options, such as public transportation.

Can I bring my bag or backpack?

To ensure the safety of the artworks and the comfort of
all visitors, we have a bag policy in place. Small bags
and backpacks are usually allowed, but larger bags may
need to be stored in the museum's coat check or locker
facilities. Please note that any bags brought into the
museum may be subject to security checks.

What are current exhibitions

We change the exhibited paintings from time to time. In
addition, we host temporary exhibitions of paintings
from other museums and galleries. You can learn about
new exhibitions and their dates on this website.
